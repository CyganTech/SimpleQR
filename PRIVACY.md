# Privacy Policy

This site is a static QR code generator. If you enable Google Tag Manager (GTM) or other
analytics, the data collected is used only to understand usage and popularity of features.

## What data may be collected
- Basic usage events (for example: QR generation, downloads, copy actions, and theme toggles).
- Device and browser metadata that analytics providers typically collect automatically.

## How the data is used
- To understand overall feature usage and improve the experience.
- To monitor aggregate traffic and identify popular features.

## What we do not do
- We do not sell or share analytics data for advertising.
- We do not collect QR code content or the URLs you enter.

## Your choices
- If you do not want analytics, remove the GTM snippet from `index.html`.
- You can also block analytics scripts in your browser.
